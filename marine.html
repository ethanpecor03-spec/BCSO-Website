"Securing Blaine County’s Waterways"

Marine Unit Overview

The Marine Unit oversees the safety and law enforcement on Blaine County’s lakes, rivers, and coastal areas. Deputies operate patrol boats and respond to water-related emergencies such as boating accidents, stranded vessels, and search and rescue missions.

Marine deputies enforce boating laws, assist with environmental protection, and coordinate with other units during multi-agency operations. Their training includes water safety, navigation, vessel operation, and emergency medical response on water.

Through proactive patrols and rapid response, the Marine Unit protects lives and property on the county’s waterways while promoting safe boating practices and maritime law compliance.
