Off-Road Unit Overview

The Off-Road Unit is specially trained to patrol Blaine County’s rugged, rural, and mountainous terrain. Deputies utilize ATVs, 4x4 trucks, dirt bikes, and other specialized vehicles to access remote areas unreachable by standard patrol cars.

Responsibilities include search and rescue operations, wildlife and environmental law enforcement, rural crime prevention, and assisting other units during emergencies. Off-Road deputies are skilled in navigation, vehicle recovery, and outdoor survival techniques to ensure public safety in challenging conditions.

This unit is essential for maintaining a law enforcement presence in the county’s wilderness, providing rapid response and professional service where traditional resources cannot operate efficiently.
