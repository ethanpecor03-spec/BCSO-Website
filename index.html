About BCSO

The Blaine County Sheriff's Office (BCSO) is the primary law enforcement agency serving Blaine County. Our deputies are sworn to uphold the law, protect life and property, and serve the citizens with fairness, professionalism, and integrity.

BCSO deputies operate in diverse environments ranging from rural towns like Grapeseed and Sandy Shores to mountain terrain near Mount Chiliad and busy highways connecting to Los Santos. Our units handle investigations, traffic enforcement, search and rescue, marine safety, and specialized crime suppression.

Community trust is the foundation of our mission. Through outreach, transparency, and accountability, we strive to create a safe environment for every resident and visitor of Blaine County.

Divisions

Patrol Unit

The core of the BCSO, handling daily patrol and emergency response.

Traffic Unit

Focused on roadway safety, DUI enforcement, and crash investigations.

Off-Road Unit

Specialized deputies for rough terrain, rescues, and rural law enforcement.

K9 Unit comming soon

Police dogs trained in narcotics, tracking, and suspect apprehension.

Marine Unit

Dedicated to boating safety and emergency response on waterways.
