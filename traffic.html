Traffic Unit Overview

The Traffic Unit specializes in maintaining safe roads throughout Blaine County. Deputies investigate traffic collisions, enforce speed limits, conduct DUI checkpoints, and address impaired or reckless driving incidents. They utilize specialized vehicles and motorcycles to patrol highways, busy intersections, and rural roads efficiently.

Traffic deputies also collaborate with other divisions during incidents such as vehicle pursuits, emergency responses, or community events. Their goal is to reduce accidents, prevent injuries, and ensure the safe flow of traffic while educating the public on responsible driving practices.

The Traffic Unit combines technical knowledge, investigative skills, and community awareness to protect motorists and pedestrians alike.
