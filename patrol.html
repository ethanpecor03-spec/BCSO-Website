Patrol Unit Overview

The Patrol Unit forms the core of the Blaine County Sheriff's Office operations. Deputies in this unit respond to emergency calls, perform proactive patrols, enforce laws, and provide a visible presence to deter crime. All new deputies begin their careers in Patrol to build a solid foundation of law enforcement experience.

Patrol deputies handle a wide range of duties including traffic enforcement, public safety checks, community outreach, and emergency response in urban, rural, and mountainous regions of Blaine County. They are trained to remain calm in high-pressure situations, make rapid decisions, and serve the community with professionalism and integrity.

The Patrol Unit is essential for maintaining order and ensuring safety across Blaine County, serving as the first responders in all incidents and emergencies.
