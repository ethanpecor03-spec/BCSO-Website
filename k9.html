K9 Unit Overview

The K9 Unit pairs highly trained police dogs with deputies to conduct narcotics detection, suspect tracking, and search-and-rescue missions. These teams are essential for high-risk operations, including armed suspect pursuits and locating missing persons.

Each canine undergoes specialized training and maintains strong bonds with their handler to perform efficiently in high-stress environments. The unit also participates in public demonstrations and community outreach, educating citizens on law enforcement canine work.

K9 deputies enhance the BCSO’s capabilities by providing unique detection and apprehension skills, strengthening public safety across the county.
